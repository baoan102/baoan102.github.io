Customer Onboarding

This project simulates a real-world scenario of a bank implementing a digital onboarding process for customers using eKYC technology. The process includes customer introduction, digital account creation, and transaction execution. "Customer Onboarding" refers to the process of approaching and welcoming new customer entities. The project focuses on this crucial initial stage in the relationship between the bank and the customer. Therefore, understanding and analyzing data related to this process is extremely important to ensure the best customer experience.

User Identification

This project focuses on using natural language processing (NLP) algorithms to determine whether two accounts on different social media platforms belong to the same person. By collecting and analyzing writing style, vocabulary, and topics from posts, the project will build a machine learning model to compare and conclude the similarity between the two accounts.
